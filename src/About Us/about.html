FAQ



1. What is 4Wheels Auction?

A platform for online auctions, 4Wheels allows users to purchase and sell a variety of vehicles, including trucks, cars, motorcycles, and more.

2. How is the auction procedure carried out?

Cars that are up for auction allow users to submit bids. The car is won by the highest bidder at the conclusion of the auction.

3. Who is eligible to bid in the auctions?

Anyone interested in bidding can register on our platform. A membership is necessary to start bidding.

4. How can I set up my account?

On our homepage, click the "Register" button, enter the necessary data, and verify your email address.

5. Is there a cost to register?

You can enter for free and add vehicles to your favorites list, but to start bidding you must purchase one of the available memberships.

6. Can I retract my bid?

A bid cannot be canceled once it has been placed. Before submitting, please make sure you are confident in your bid.
